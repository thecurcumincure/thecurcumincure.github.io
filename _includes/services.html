Curcumin & Gut Eubiosis

DYSBIOSIS: an imbalance/change in bacterial composition in the gut
Effects: can range from digestive problems to chronic diseases as it impacts digestive, immune, and neurological systems

increase in phyla such as proteobacteria, spirochaetes, and deferribacteres

EUBIOSIS: a state of healthy microbial balance in the gut
Effects: supports metabolic functions, protects against pathogens, provides essential nutrients

curcumin decreases proteobacteria, spirochaetes, and deferribacteres

curcumin also increases firmicutes (associated with general gut health), and actinobacteria (antibiotic and anticancer qualities)
