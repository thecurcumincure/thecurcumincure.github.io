Awards

This project was submitted to local science fairs and advanced far!

1) 1st place at Greater San Diego Science & Engineering Fair (2021–2024)

After presenting this project at the local San Diego science fair, Tanya won 1st place consecutively and advanced to the state level at the California Science & Engineering Fair.

2) BD “Advancing the World of Health” Award of $1000 (2022)

Tanya received a special award for her research on curcumin from BD Biosciences.

3) Association for Women in STEM Special Award of $100 (2023)

Tanya received a special award from this professional society at the San Diego science fair in 2023.

4) 1st place in Senior Division, San Diego Veterinary Medical Association Special Award of $100 (2023, 2024, 2025)

Tanya was recognized as a 3-time winner of the Veterinary Medical Association’s special award. She was also invited to shadow veterinarians at the San Diego Humane Society.

5) 2nd Place ($1500) at California Southern Junior Science and Humanities Symposium (JSHS)

Tanya won 2nd place in Southern California’s regional JSHS and advanced as an oral presenter to the National JSHS, one of 98 oral presenters nationally.

6) Honorable Mention, Scripps Research “Young Scientist” Award

Tanya was recognized with an honorable mention for the Scripps Research special award at the San Diego science fair.
